How the "Ugly Dark" Claimed the "White Princess"

The Confrontation

Nanzouses Gravity Manipulation to completely immobilize Karui for 1 full minute. While she suffers death pain from Wave Style internal attacks, he mocks her cruelly: "Ugly weak darky," trying to break her spirit with humiliation.

The 5-Meter Dead Zone

While locked, Karui studies him. She notices the 5-meter gravity dead zone, realizes his Wind Style would kill them both at close range, understands he has zero taijutsu/kenjutsu ability. She memorizes his cooldown timers.

The Trap
Helpless Without Powers

Nanzo tries Wind Style attacks, but they're mid/long-range only. Using them close would trigger absorption (Vortex) or massive destruction--suicide for both. He is trapped: no gravity (30m cd), no wave (15m cd), no wind (suicide), no physical skills.

The Claim
The Bite Mark

The moment gravity releases, despite death pain from Wave damage, Karui explodes forward using Killer B's flash-speed training. She doesn't use her sword--she BITS his left neck, sinking teeth deep, placing permanent scar as her claim.

"He's mine. I claimed him when he was helpless."

Aftermath
Acceptance & Transformation

Nanzo accepts defeat and her as his partner. Over time, they accept true feelings and fall in love. Post-marriage transformation: he becomes mature, worthy husband; she becomes deeply loving, respectful wife. She trains him in taijutsu/kenjutsu, awakening Wind Style: Paralyze Sword.
